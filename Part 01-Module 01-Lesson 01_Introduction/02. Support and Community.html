Support and community

We want you to have all of the support you need to succeed in this program. Here are the places to go when you have a question, get stuck, or just want to connect with others.

Take a few minutes to check them out so you know what's available and how to access it!

Student Hub

As part of your Nanodegree program, you're now part of Student Hub, where you can chat with your classmates and mentor. Just look for this icon at the bottom of the menu (in the lower left corner):

In Student Hub, you'll be able to:

  • Collaborate directly with classmates working on the same projects.

  • Request direct support from a qualified mentor by @mentioning them.

  • Join fellow lifelong learners in informative group chats and interactive Ask-Me-Anythings (AMAs) with industry experts.
